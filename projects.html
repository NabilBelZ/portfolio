MamaBox Super-Application Development and Security
This project centers on the development and security of MamaBox, a super-application designed to support future and young mothers before and after childbirth. The work involved full-stack development and the implementation of robust security measures to ensure data protection and safe digital interactions.

Key contributions include the design of the Community module, enabling private chats, group participation, event management, and reporting mechanisms, as well as the Marketplace module, allowing partners to create online stores, sell products, and manage revenue-sharing with MamaBox.

In addition to these developments, the project integrated advanced features such as an AI-powered chatbot for maternal assistance, video-consultation with healthcare professionals, and an intelligent baby crib capable of monitoring infants. The objective was to deliver a secure, scalable, and innovative platform combining software and hardware to enhance the maternal and postnatal care experience.
